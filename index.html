Premium Beneng Taro
From Bogor, Indonesia

Bogor Taro Leaves is a local agricultural-based business from Bogor, Indonesia, specialising in Beneng taro leaves and taro-based products. We focus on delivering natural, high-quality raw materials processed with care and consistency to meet both domestic and international market needs.

We work closely with local farmers to ensure sustainable sourcing, stable supply, and product quality from harvest to packaging.

About Us

Bogor Taro Leaf is a Bogor-based agricultural business specialising in dried sliced Beneng taro leaves. Rooted in local farming traditions, we aim to bring the value of Beneng taro to a wider market through responsible sourcing and careful processing.

We collaborate directly with local farmers in Bogor to ensure that each stage of production, from cultivation and harvesting to slicing and drying, is handled with attention to quality and consistency. This close partnership allows us to maintain product traceability, stable supply, and fair practices within the local farming community.

Our processing methods prioritise natural techniques without unnecessary chemical additives, ensuring that the original characteristics of Beneng taro leaves are preserved. Through proper handling and hygienic packaging, we deliver products that are suitable for long-term storage and various applications.

Bogor Taro Leaf is committed to transparency, sustainability, and long-term growth. We strive to become a reliable partner for customers seeking authentic Beneng taro leaf products sourced responsibly from Bogor.
